# Workflow: Hackathon Pitch Preparation
1. Review the entire codebase and the 'walkthrough.md' artifact.
2. Generate a 'pitch_deck.md' containing:
   - THE HOOK: A 1-sentence emotional problem (e.g., "Arjun loses 4 hours to traffic...").
   - THE NOVELTY: Why this is an "Agent," not just an "App."
   - THE STACK: Mention Go for performance and Gemini 3.0 for reasoning.
3. Use the Browser Sub-agent to take 4 high-quality screenshots of the best UI features.
4. Draft a 2-minute live demo script that highlights the AI's "Agentic" decision-making.
